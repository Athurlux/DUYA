Our Destinations

Discover East Africa with Duya Discoveries

🇺🇬 Uganda – The Pearl of Africa

Uganda is home to incredible biodiversity, from lush jungles to vast savannahs. Highlights: Gorilla & chimpanzee trekking in Bwindi and Kibale, wildlife safaris in Murchison Falls and Queen Elizabeth National Park, cultural experiences, and adventure sports like white-water rafting in Jinja.

🇹🇿 Tanzania – Land of Kilimanjaro & Serengeti

Tanzania is Africa’s safari capital, offering breathtaking landscapes and wildlife spectacles. Highlights: The Great Migration in Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Mount Kilimanjaro, and Zanzibar’s island charm.

🇰🇪 Kenya – Classic Safari Country

Kenya combines thrilling safaris with rich traditions and coastal escapes. Highlights: Big Five safaris in Masai Mara, Amboseli with views of Kilimanjaro, Tsavo National Park, and authentic Maasai cultural experiences.

🏝 Zanzibar – Spice Island Paradise

Zanzibar is a romantic and cultural escape surrounded by turquoise waters. Highlights: White sandy beaches, Stone Town (a UNESCO site), spice plantation tours, and unforgettable sunsets.

🇷🇼 Rwanda – Land of a Thousand Hills

Rwanda offers stunning scenery and rich cultural heritage in a safe, welcoming environment. Highlights: Gorilla trekking in Volcanoes National Park, Lake Kivu, and Kigali’s vibrant culture and history.
